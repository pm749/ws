About me

I am a post-doctoral researcher at the Chair of Public Economics. My reseach is about household finance, wealth inequality and intergenerational mobility. I received my PhD from the University of Cyprus in June 2026.

Working papers and work in progress

  • Social circumstances and inequality of opportunity. (with Andros Kourtellos and Yiguo Sun)
